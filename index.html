About Me

Turning data into
systems that deliver

I am a results-driven Data Analyst with over 5 years of experience in data analysis, business intelligence, and applied analytics. My background combines Physics (BSc, Second Class Upper) and Information Science (MSc), giving me both quantitative rigour and systems thinking.

I don't just analyze data — I build the infrastructure around it. From automated KPI pipelines that deliver executive reports to stakeholders' inboxes every morning without anyone lifting a finger, to enrolment prediction tools that help universities plan ahead, to machine learning systems that flag at-risk students before they drop out. My work is designed to be used, not just presented.

I also build AI-powered products that solve real problems in the real world. TraderBot, my WhatsApp business assistant, runs 24/7 serving Nigerian market traders — recording sales, tracking stock, managing debts, and generating business summaries entirely through natural conversation, with zero infrastructure cost.

I am open to full-time, remote, hybrid, and international opportunities in Data Analysis, Business Intelligence Engineering, and ML-powered analytics. I bring research-oriented thinking, strong communication skills, and a proven track record of delivering production-grade systems that solve real problems.
